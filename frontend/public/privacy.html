Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2025

What HealthStitch Is

HealthStitch is a personal, self-hosted health data dashboard. It pulls together data from wearable devices — currently WHOOP, with Apple Watch and others planned — so you can see your readiness, sleep, recovery, and workout trends in one place.

This is a personal project, not a commercial product. It runs on hardware owned and operated by the user. There is no cloud service, no hosted backend, and no multi-tenant infrastructure.

What Data We Collect

HealthStitch accesses the following data from connected devices via their official APIs:

  • Sleep records (duration, stages, sleep/wake times)
  • Recovery and readiness scores
  • Heart rate and heart rate variability measurements
  • Workout and activity data (strain, duration, calories)
  • Body measurements (e.g., resting heart rate, SpO2, respiratory rate)

HealthStitch also stores OAuth tokens required to authenticate with device APIs (e.g., WHOOP). These tokens are stored locally and used solely to fetch your health data.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used for one purpose: displaying your personal health trends on your own dashboard. Specifically:

  • Computing baselines and trend comparisons across devices
  • Generating your morning readiness check-in
  • Showing workout load, sleep quality, and recovery over time

Your data is never used for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose other than showing it back to you.

Where Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your own server in a SQLite database. Nothing is sent to a cloud service, external database, or third-party server. There is no remote backup or replication unless you configure it yourself.

Data Sharing

HealthStitch does not share your data with anyone. Period. There are:

  • No third-party analytics or tracking scripts
  • No advertising networks
  • No data brokers or partners
  • No telemetry or usage reporting

The only external network calls HealthStitch makes are to the device APIs (e.g., WHOOP's API at api.prod.whoop.com) to fetch your data.

Security

Because HealthStitch is self-hosted, security depends on your own server configuration. The application itself takes these precautions:

  • OAuth tokens are stored server-side and never exposed to the browser
  • API endpoints require authentication
  • No default credentials — you configure your own
  • HTTPS is supported and recommended for any network-exposed deployment

Third-Party Services

HealthStitch integrates with the following third-party APIs:

  • WHOOP — to sync sleep, recovery, workout, and body measurement data. WHOOP's own privacy policy applies to data on their platform: whoop.com/privacy

HealthStitch does not send your data to these services — it only reads data from them using your authorized credentials.

Data Retention

Your data is retained in the local database indefinitely, or until you delete it. You have full control over the database and can delete any or all data at any time. Revoking a device's OAuth connection stops future data sync but does not automatically delete previously synced data.

Your Rights

Since you host HealthStitch yourself, you have complete control over your data. You can view, export, modify, or delete any data at any time by accessing the SQLite database directly.

Children's Privacy

HealthStitch is a personal tool and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised. Since this is a self-hosted application, you control when and whether to update.
