2. SERVICE DESCRIPTION
HealthStitch is a personal health data aggregation tool that consolidates metrics from multiple wearable devices into a unified dashboard. Features include:
• Syncing health data from WHOOP and Apple Watch
• Displaying heart rate variability (HRV), sleep, strain, and recovery metrics
• Computing per-source baselines and trend analysis
• Importing health data from CSV and XML exports
• Device comparison with independent baselines
