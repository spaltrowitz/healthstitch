### Copy Voice & Naming (Book, 2025-01-30)

**Decision:** All user-facing text uses a warm, personal voice — like a health-savvy friend. Technical terms get plain-language aliases with device attribution in parentheses where needed.

**Key choices:**
- App name: **HealthStitch** (not "Unified Health Dashboard")
- Tab labels are task-oriented: "Today's Readiness", "Trends", "Apple vs WHOOP", "Workouts"
- Missing data shown as em-dash "—", never "--"
- Loading states are conversational and specific
- Error messages lead with what went wrong in human terms, then append technical detail
- Chart legends attribute the device, not the algorithm: "Apple Watch (SDNN)" not "Apple SDNN"

**Rationale:** Users aren't clinicians. They want to know "am I ready today?" not "what is my RMSSD?". The copy should help them understand without dumbing things down.
